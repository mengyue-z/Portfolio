About Me

Hello, my name is Mengyue Zhang. I have a M.S. degree in Marketing Analytics from the University of Maryland and I have been working as a Marketing Specialist for over 2 years since graduated. Maintaining and updating the website is one of my daily jobs, so I did some self-learning of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to help make the website fancier and increase the interaction with users. Out of interest, I started a Java programming lesson online and had a chance to know more about computer science field. I find myself really enjoy coding and therefore, with the great passion, I decided to try switching my career to become a full-stack developer.

I have been admitted to the MS of Software Development program in Boston University, which, I believe, will give me some professional guidance and help me with my career change. To get prepared before going to School, I am now enrolled in the George Washington University Full Stack Coding Bootcamp. My goal is to advance necessary technical skills and practice my understanding of the full-stack development field in this intensive program and thus become employer-ready.
